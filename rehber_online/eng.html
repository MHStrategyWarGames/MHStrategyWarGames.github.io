GENERAL GAMEPLAY
● The Goal of the game is to conquer the whole world by making land operations or using the secret agency.
● You can use the options related to economy, security and country management on the game screen.
● To move to the next day, you must use the arrow at the bottom right of the game screen.
● Daily income and daily population are added to all states in day transitions.
● When the day is done, other states may make offers to your country or random world events may occur.
● The decisions you make on offers and situations can affect your economy, population, and relationships with other governments.

ECONOMY
● You can earn money by investing in your country.
● You can earn money by choosing a tax rate.
● You can earn money by processing the mineral resources found.
● You can make investments by sending your business people to other countries.
● You earn daily income from public institutions.
● Factory and Industry research earns you extra income.
● You earn extra money from every tourist who comes to your country.
● You can support your economy by drawing loans.
● If you conquer countries, you will win a one-time grand prize.
● 100% of the daily income of the countries you occupy is added to you.
● (You can check it from Statistics - Conquest Proceeds section.)
● You can earn money by performing operations from the Homeland Security section.
● You can make money by doing cyber attacks on other countries.
● You can earn money by declaring a National Mobilization.
● You can make money using your Secret Service.

OPERATION CENTER
● You can invade countries with ground operations.
● If you succeed in the ground campaign, you will earn capture points.
● If the invasion point reaches 100%, the country will be conquered.
● You can weaken the army of the enemy country with air strikes.
● You can make cyber attacks on other countries.
● You can use the extra options by activating the consulate building.
● You can get military, economic and detailed reports by placing spies in countries.
● You can make friendship agreements with the countries you are at war with.
● You can do all attacks with permissions.
● You can apply embargo to countries.

DOMESTIC PRODUCTION
● You can produce the product you want from the production section.
● You must send countries trade requests to sell your products.
● You can negotiate prices for products that other countries want to buy.
● Only friendly countries want to buy products from you.
● The more product variety you have, the more chances you have to trade.
● By improving your trade relations with other countries, you can increase the prices of your offers.

PERSONAL SECURITY
● The countries you are at war with can assassinate you.
● You should consider the Advisor's Report.
● To protect yourself from assassinations, you should appoint a highly protective counselor.
● You also need to upgrade support packages.

SECRET ORGANIZATION
● You can start riots in other countries by establishing the Secret Service.
● You can assassinate with the Secret Service.
● You can only use trained personnel in operations.
● Your trained staff and weapons will increase your operational strength.

ASSEMBLY and ELECTIONS
● Elections are held every 365 days.
● To win the elections, you must get the highest number of votes.
● If you are successful in the choices made, you will earn extra money.
● Make laws, rallies, distribute money, interview to win votes.
● If you lose the elections, you have to make a military intervention within 8 days.
● If the military intervention fails, the game is over.

(PREMIUM) GLOBAL ALLIANCES
● You can form your own alliance or enter existing alliances.
● You can hold meetings with allied states.
● You can earn money by completing the requests of the allied states.
● Alliance research activates extra options.
● You can strengthen your defense by using alliance productions.
● You can conduct joint operations with allied states.
● If you attack member states in the same alliance, you can be expelled from the alliance.

(PREMIUM) UNITED NATIONS
● You can vote 5 different resolutions using the United Nations.
● Financial support or economic sanction decisions can be taken for other countries.
● New decisions cannot be made until active decisions are over.
● Only non-conquered countries vote in decisions.

(PREMIUM) HEAVY INDUSTRY
● 5 different customized weapons and equipment can be produced by heavy industry.
● Only friendly countries with trade requests can send you a trade request.
● Produced weapons cannot be used in battles.

WIN AND LOSE
● If you conquer all countries 100%, you win the game.
● If your money is less than -20,000,000 the game is over.
● If happiness is less than 0, the game is over.
